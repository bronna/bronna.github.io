In a recent talk by John Burn-Murdoch he presented a study showing Republicans were 3-4x more likely to accept climate science when presented with a chart vs. a paragraph of statistics. That’s a stunning difference, and I’m sure goes for both sides. People tend to automatically perceive charts as more objective, and, apparently, persuasive.

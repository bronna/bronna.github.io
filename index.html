Brianna Wilson -

Designer & Engineer

Making data human-friendly. Using technologies like 3D, web interactivity, and AR, Brianna explores novel and engaging ways to visualize information. Her work has appeared in the Washington Post, Nightingale Journal of Data Visualization, and more. Her training started at Stanford in the Product Design program, after which she worked as a product design engineer at Apple on the first several iPads. Since then, she's started a sustainable toy company, completed the Lede Program in Data Journalism at Columbia, and continues her work in data journalism and communication design.

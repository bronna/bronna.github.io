// ABOUT

Brianna Wilson creates data experiences that shine light on society and the natural world. Previously, she was a designer of children’s toys, a Product Design Engineer on Apple’s iPad team, and a graduate of Stanford’s Product Design Engineering program and Columbia's Lede Program in Data Journalism. She’s based out of Portland, Oregon, USA, and in her free time loves to design sustainable landscapes, take dance classes, and go hiking.

3D WebGL Landscapes

  • WebGL
  • Three.js
  • Shaders
  • Javascript
  • 3D

An experimental project in response to a creative coding challenge in the Elevate Dataviz community. Given my love of 3D, I turned to WebGL to make a dimensional landscape come to life. I came out of this particular project with a deeper understanding of how shaders work, and how to combine multiple shaders together. Both landscapes are based on the same simplex noise function in a vertex shader (the underlying geometry), but have different fragment shaders (the colors of the pixels).

Higher-Income Californians Choose Longer Commutes

  • PYTHON
  • PANDAS
  • JUPYTER
  • ILLUSTRATOR

The US government sheds massive amounts of datasets that look, at first glance, like unending walls of numbers and symbols. Challenging myself to find insights in a random dataset, however, I used Python and Pandas in a Jupyter notebook to find takeaways in a set of spreadsheets from NREL. For me, this exercise solidified the principle of “simplify and focus” in data visualization. In other words, take out the extraneous, distracting markings in a chart, and highlight what the main takeaways are.

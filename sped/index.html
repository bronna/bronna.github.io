Definitions

inclusive: in a regular classroom for more than 80% of the day

semi-inclusive: in a regular classroom for between 40% and 80% of the day

non-inclusive: in a regular classroom for less than 40% of the day

separate: in a setting other than school (e.g. hospital, juvenile detention, etc.)

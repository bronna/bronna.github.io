How Inclusive is Special Education in Your State?

Find data on a state's school districts and local education agencies. Currently showing the state of Oregon. California and Washington coming soon.

Select a school district or local education agency:

IEP: Individualized Education Plan, formed by a team of a student's teachers, therapists, guardians/parents, and school representatives to evaluate what services a child will need to succeed in the least restrictive environment
Inclusive: in a regular classroom for more than 80% of the day
Semi-inclusive: in a regular classroom for 40% to 80% of the day
Non-inclusive: in a regular classroom for less than 40% of the day
Separate: in a non-school setting, such as a hospital or detention center

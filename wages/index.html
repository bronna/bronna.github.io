How Has Worker & CEO Pay Changed Over Time?

  • Javascript
  • D3.js
  • Illustrator

Sensing the growing inequality around the Bay Area, where I lived for many years, I found data from the Economic Policy Institute and used it to make an initial visualization in D3. Animating it opened my eyes to the challenge of visualizing exponential growth rates. It's not intuitive to grasp. Though a visualization helps with understanding, it has to be done carefully to show what exponential growth actually means. What I like about this approach is that the y-axis scales over time, giving a sense of how what looks like a huge rate of growth at one point in time, can end up being minuscule compared to future growth rates.

I decided to try to make a more interactive version of the visualization. This was one of my first deep dives into Javascript, and taught me many things, including the joys of text wrapping in SVG (:P). In this version, I took a more storytelling perspective, showing how these numbers are directly relevant to someone's life.
